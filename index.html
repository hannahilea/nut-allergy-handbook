6 places we've deemed contextually safe, given the below details. We always check the menu and with the staff.
Place Link Details Last checked
Vinal General Store [link] The general store is safe for sandwiches; the softserve is not (sometimes they put nutty flavors in the machine). The next-door Vinal Bakery is not safe. Jun 2025
Neighborhood Restaurant [link] Omelets, egg/potato dishes only: they have a banana nutella crepe, so we avoid the pancake/crepe/waffle section and the bakery. Jun 2025
Petra [link] Safe as of last visit, but the owner said they are planning to add muhammara soon (which contains walnuts); when they do, we will stop going here. Kitchen is way too small to plausibly prevent cross-contamination. Jun 2025
Bloc [link] Coffee drinks only! The baked goods and sandwiches are not safe: the sandwich components are prepared in the kitchen of Forge which makes muhammara (which contains walnuts) so even though the menu might look safe, as far as we know it is not. Jun 2025
Forge Baking Co. & Ice Cream Bar [link] Coffee drinks only! The kitchen makes muhammara (contains walnuts), the bakery makes various nutty pastries. Jun 2025
Page & Leaf Cafe [link] Coffee drinks only! Baked goods, food, and smoothies are not. (Same owners, shared bakery, and menu concerns as as Bloc, Diesel, and Forge) Jun 2025
